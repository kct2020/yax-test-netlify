About Learning Yax

Learning Yax is my story about learning what Yax is, how to adapt it for my specific needs, and how to improve it to create useful, usable website templates for other people.

This is inspired by the Yax creator - Daniel Kehoe. Especially in response to his Yax worklog entry:

Yax had 2575 new visitors. From this traffic, I had 128 visitors subscribe to a Stackless Newsletter I created, with some enthusiastic feedback. Among the visitors, 96 people tried deploying a template with “Try Yax.” I checked GitHub repos and no users had done anything more with their Yax websites than just a test.
